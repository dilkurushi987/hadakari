About Us

Hadakari is a garment manufacturer, retailer, and online retailer offering a wide range of brands from high-end to exclusive collections. ...We are a family of fashion lovers who are inspired to create mind-blowing women's apparel designs from the most luxurious materials that are both comfortable and fashionable.
Hadakari, which was established in the year 2017 and offered the highest quality designs in accordance with international standards, has come a long way. We intend to broaden our brand portfolio into the global fashion market as a constant backup thanks to our years of experience. We cordially invite foreign clients to embrace our distinctive women's clothing designs. Sri Lanka's Ampara is home to our flagship store. As we gradually expand into the fashion industry, our top aim is to continue to grasp the preferences, fashion trends, and personal preferences of our cherished clients on a national and international scale.
We are one of the leading women's clothing brands in Sri Lanka and will keep going till we reach the top level thanks to the established base, the ongoing search for ways to expand, and the sharpening of our talents. Furthermore, we want to empower women everywhere by helping them feel confident in their own sense of style and fashion. Hadakari is a daring, forward-thinking clothing line with goals and objectives that are grounded in reality.
Every product we design, produce, and market is influenced by our customers as well as by positive global influences like social media, street style, and popular culture. This has resulted in the creation of a brand that embodies everything it means to be a woman on the go in the fast-paced world of today while offering extraordinary styles and designs at a competitive price. It's rapid fashion, not simply fast fashion. We regularly provide our customers with enticing new collections and fashions.
